This section demonstrates my engagement with cybersecurity professionals from the industry to gain insights into real-world projects and current practices.

## 1. Rama Indrawan – Troppus IT & Management

### Description
I engaged with Rama Indrawan, who is currently the Head of Support Services – Infrastructure & Website Management at Troppus IT & Management in Perth. Through this discussion, I gained insight into his role in managing infrastructure and ensuring that organisational systems remain secure while supporting business operations.

Rama explained that one of his latest projects involves performing security uplift initiatives for customers. This process focuses on improving the overall security posture of organisations by aligning their systems with recognised cybersecurity frameworks such as the Essential Eight, Security Operations Centre (SOC) practices, and ISO standards. These frameworks provide structured guidelines to identify vulnerabilities, strengthen controls, and improve compliance, ultimately enhancing protection against cyber threats.

## 2. Joshua Qwek – Elevate 137

### Description
I also engaged with Joshua Qwek, who is the Director of Cyber Advisory & Architecture at Elevate 137. During the discussion, I learned about his role in advising organisations on cybersecurity strategies and designing secure system architectures to protect critical systems.

Joshua explained that one of his current projects focuses on Operational Technology (OT) Monitoring. This involves monitoring industrial systems such as those used in manufacturing, energy, and critical infrastructure to detect unusual or malicious activity. Since OT environments control physical processes, they are highly sensitive to cyber attacks. OT monitoring enables organisations to detect threats in real time and respond quickly to prevent disruptions or damage.
